About Aiman

Aiman Razali is a distinguished violinist with over 16 years of professional experience, specializing in classical and contemporary repertoire. A graduate of the Akademi Seni Budaya dan Warisan ( ASWARA ), Aiman has performed with multiple bands across Malaysia.

His passion for music extends beyond the concert hall, as he regularly performs at weddings, corporate events, and private gatherings, bringing the elegance and emotion of classical violin to every occasion.
